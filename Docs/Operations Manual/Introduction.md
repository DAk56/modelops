### System Overview

**ModelOps** is an internal framework developed and maintained by **Maximus** to provide secure, scalable access to artificial intelligence (AI) services. It exposes standardized endpoints via the **API Gateway**, enabling seamless integration of AI capabilities into client applications.

#### **Role of Admin APIs in the Lifecycle**
 Admin APIs play a foundational role in the ModelOps lifecycle by enabling the onboarding, configuration, and governance of tenants, users, and AI models. They serve as the entry point for initializing environments, managing access, and ensuring operational readiness for model deployment and usage.

#### **Intended Audience**
 These APIs are designed for administrators and internal engineering teams responsible for onboarding, configuring, and managing tenants, users, and AI models within the ModelOps ecosystem.
