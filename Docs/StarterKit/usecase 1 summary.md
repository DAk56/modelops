Analyze a candidate’s resume (PDF) to identify the most relevant job roles and provide fit scores, matched skills, skill gaps, and targeted improvement guidance.

## When to Use
Use this when:

- Recruiters want consistent fit signals across large volumes.
- Automated triage of incoming resumes to suggested job families/roles is needed.



## Prerequisites

- Python 3.9+
- Tenant access: Tenant is onboarded and models access is granted.
- Resume is already available and is encoded in Base64 format.
